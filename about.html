About Stuga Studio

We create professional UI tools that make high-quality video creation accessible to everyone

Our Mission

Empowering creators with powerful, intuitive tools

At Stuga Studio, we believe that everyone deserves access to professional-grade video creation tools. Our mission is to break down the barriers between creative vision and execution by providing intuitive, browser-based applications that deliver studio-quality results.


We started Stuga Studio to solve a common problem: creating professional videos and animations shouldn't require expensive software, powerful hardware, or years of training. Our tools are designed to be powerful yet simple, allowing creators to focus on their craft rather than technical complexities.

Every tool we build is carefully crafted with real-world use cases in mind. Whether you're a social media manager creating Instagram reels, a designer showcasing your portfolio, or a presenter needing professional countdown timers, our tools are built to deliver exceptional results with minimal learning curves.


We're committed to continuous improvement, regularly updating our tools based on user feedback and emerging creative trends. Our goal is to remain at the forefront of browser-based creative technology, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in a web browser.
